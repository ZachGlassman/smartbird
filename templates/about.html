Who created smartbird?

Hi I'm Zach and smartbird is my capstone project as a Data Incubator Fellow. smartbird has been lovingly crafted in the Bay Area in order to help people have a better experience flying.

I decided to create smartbird after flying accross the country regularly and noticing that that there were certain "things" I started to value more than just the price of the flight. I wanted to quantify these "things" and so I made smartbird. smartbird quantifies all of the "things".
